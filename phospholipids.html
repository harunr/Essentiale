What are Essential Phospholipids?

First let’s talk about Phospholipids. EPLs are the building blocks of all cell membranes, including liver cells. [5] EPLs are responsible for the repair of the cell wall [6] as well as the protection and regeneration of the cells. [32]
When liver cells are damaged, their cell membranes lose phospholipids. If these phospholipids are not replaced, it may result in decreased liver functioning or worse, liver disease. [32]
While the body can produce some phospholipid compounds, others must be supplied by the diet. Phospholipids that can only be obtained through dietary intake are called “ESSENTIAL PHOSPHOLIPIDS” [22]

Food containing EPLs: [32]

soya beans
egg yolk & milk
seafood
